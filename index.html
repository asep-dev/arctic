Who Can Go?

This is important: a snowmobiling excursion with Arctic VIP is for EVERYBODY! Ladies, gentlemen, young and old. There are no real limits, except you have to be 16 to be allowed to drive. To drive a snowmobile is not difficult at all, and with proper instructions and due caution, this is a very safe and comfortable activity. No prior experience is necessary.

However, your guide will adjust the tour to your skills. Speed, terrain and length is adjusted so that each group is satisfied, whether family tours or adrenaline junkies.
